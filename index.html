In high school, I participated in a competition engineering team, building and competing with trebuchets in both distance and accuracy, Vex robots, and a car for the All-American Soap Box Derby; as well as serving as a club officer. My senior year brought my most proud moments. While I did not compete with trebuchets, I did build a ballista (watch our first test fire!
